Welcome to the CIP Comment Tool and Interactive Map!

This map serves as a location for the public to provide its feedback on CIP projects in Massachusetts. As we take a look at the concept for the corridor, please keep in mind that the design is meant to accommodate the long-term evolution of Route 20 based on planned new development in this part of the Town of Shrewsbury. You may provide comments on existing conditions in the corridor, but please consider adding comments on how the long-term concept works for you and the town, including how it plans to address land use and transportation goals.
